HOW IT RUNS

Place. Wire. Run. Ship.

  1. STEP 01

    Place nodes

    Drag typed nodes onto the infinite dotted canvas.

  2. STEP 02

    Wire top-to-bottom

    Connect outputs to inputs with bezier edges. Fan out with Parallel.

  3. STEP 03

    Press Run

    The chain executes node→node; Review nodes pause for Approve / Reject.

  4. STEP 04

    Merge & Deploy

    Fan back in at Merge, then ship from a Deploy node.
