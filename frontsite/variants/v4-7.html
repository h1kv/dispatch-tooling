voice control

Talk to your canvas.

A particle VoiceOrb listens, thinks, and speaks — editing the graph as you talk. Say it; watch the node appear.

transcript
you:

The VoiceOrb cycles through four states: idle (gentle breathing), listening (reacting to your voice), thinking (turbulent processing), and speaking (pulsing as it responds). It can add, remove and rewire nodes on the canvas from spoken instructions.
