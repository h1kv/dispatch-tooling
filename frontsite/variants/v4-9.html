voice control

Talk to your canvas.

A particle VoiceOrb listens, thinks, and speaks — editing the graph as you describe it. No mouse required.

listening
you: “”

The VoiceOrb cycles through four states: idle (gently breathing), listening (reacting to your voice), thinking (turbulent), and speaking (pulsing). Spoken commands edit the graph — for example, adding a Review node after Investigate.

Reviewnew

The orb edits a live graph. Speak a change; the node fades in where you asked.
