handed down the chain.

When a node finishes, it hands its full context — and the baton of control — to the next node. The chain crosses providers without glue code: OpenAI investigates, Anthropic plans, Google designs.

agentic voice control

Talk to your canvas.

A particle VoiceOrb listens, thinks, and speaks — editing the graph as you talk. Say what you want; nodes appear, wire themselves, and run.

The VoiceOrb has four states: idle (a gentle breathing sphere), listening (it ripples toward your voice), thinking (turbulent, high-energy motion), and speaking (a pulsing blue-to-accent envelope).
