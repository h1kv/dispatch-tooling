agentic voice control

Talk to your canvas.

A particle orb listens, thinks, and speaks — editing the graph as you describe it. No mouse required.

The voice orb cycles through four states: idle (a gentle breathing sphere), listening (it ripples toward your cursor as it captures speech), thinking (turbulent high-frequency motion while it reasons), and speaking (rhythmic pulses as it responds and edits the graph).
