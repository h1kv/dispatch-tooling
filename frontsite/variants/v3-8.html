/ 02 — no lock-in

Hotswap models per node.

/ 07 — execution

How a chain runs.

01

Place nodes

Drop nodes onto the infinite canvas from the toolbox.

02

Wire them

Connect top-to-bottom. Fan out with Parallel, recombine with Merge.

03

Press Run

Each node executes and passes its output to the next in the chain.

04

Review & ship

Pause at a Review node to Approve or Reject, then Deploy.
