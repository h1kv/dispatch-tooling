// agentic handoff

Context and control, handed off.

Each node passes its full context and control to the next — automatically, even across providers. A run started on OpenAI can hand off to Anthropic, then Google, without glue code.

// agentic voice control

Talk to your canvas.

A VoiceOrb listens, thinks and speaks — then edits the graph for you. Say it out loud; the chain rewires itself.

idle

The VoiceOrb has four states: idle while waiting, listening while you speak, thinking while it plans the edit, and speaking while it responds.
