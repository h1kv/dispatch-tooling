Mission

Celebrating Ireland’s Next Generation of AI Innovators

Over five days, 24 Transition Year and 5th Year students explored how AI can accelerate startups, research, creative projects, and technical experimentation.

The showcase is the culmination of Patch x OpenAI Young Builders Week: a community gathering where students present what they built, share lessons learned, and connect with mentors, founders, technologists, and Ireland’s local technology community.

About Patch

A community for exceptional young builders

Patch is Ireland’s community and accelerator for exceptional young technologists, scientists, and entrepreneurs.

Through mentorship, ambitious projects, and a supportive peer network, Patch helps young people build, experiment, and lead. Its programs emphasize real products, startup experimentation, research projects, technical skills, ambition, and leadership development.

Organizers: Patch Youth Accelerator, Katelyn Davis, and Kai Manning.
